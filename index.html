    FAQ

    Common questions

    Here are some of the most common questions that we get.

    How do I play Rock, Paper, Scissors?

    To play Rock, Paper, Scissors, simply choose either rock, paper, or scissors and see if you win against the computer's choice

    Is there a strategy to win at Rock, Paper, Scissors?

    Rock, Paper, Scissors is a game of chance, so there is no guaranteed strategy to win every time. It's all about luck!

    Can I play Rock, Paper, Scissors on my mobile device?

    Yes, our Rock, Paper, Scissors game is mobile-friendly and can be played on any device with an internet connection.

    Is Rock, Paper, Scissors available in different languages?

    Currently, our game is available only in English. We are working on adding more language options in the future.

    How can I contact support if I encounter any issues with the game?

    If you have any issues or questions regarding the game, please reach out to our support team at support@rockpaperscissors.com
